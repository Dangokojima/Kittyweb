By requesting a commission, you declare that you have read and agree to all the terms below.

1. Commission Process

  • To request a commission, the client must provide a clear description of the order and visual references whenever possible.
  • The artist reserves the right to refuse or cancel any order if they feel uncomfortable with the content or the client's conduct.
  • If the order is canceled by the artist before the work begins, the client will receive a full refund.

2. Queue and Delivery Timeline

  • Commissions are produced on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • The client will be informed of their position in the queue whenever necessary.
  • The average delivery time is up to 4 weeks after the project starts.
  • Depending on the complexity of the art, the timeframe may be longer or shorter and will be stated in the initial quote.
  • During the process, the client will receive updates on the artwork's progress.

3. Revisions and Alterations

  • Significant changes can only be requested during the sketch phase.
  • After the sketch is approved, only minor modifications or color adjustments can be requested.
  • Major changes after this phase may result in an additional fee.

4. Payment

  • Payment must be made before the work begins.
  • For larger projects, a 50% upfront and 50% after sketch approval option may be offered.
  • Accepted payment methods: PIX and Bank Transfer.

5. Additional Fees

Some orders may have extra costs depending on complexity:

  • Detailed backgrounds
  • Multiple accessories or elements
  • Additional animals or creatures

Specific fees may also apply:

  • Priority (Rush Fee): +50% of the total value
  • Commercial use (Merchandising): +100% of the base price
  • Privacy / Non-disclosure: +10%

6. Copyright and Art Usage

The artist retains all copyrights to the artwork.

The client may:

  • Use the art for personal purposes
  • Post the art on social media with credit to the artist

The client may not:

  • Remove the artist's signature
  • Modify the art without permission
  • Resell the art
  • Use the art for commercial purposes without acquiring commercial rights

7. Commercial Use

If the art is used for:

  • Products
  • Merchandise
  • Sales
  • Direct or indirect monetization

It is mandatory to acquire commercial rights, which involve an additional fee.

8. Art Posting

The artist reserves the right to:

  • Post progress shots (WIPs)
  • Publish the final artwork on social media
  • Include the artwork in their portfolio

If the client requests confidentiality, a privacy fee will be applied.

9. Refund Policy

Refunds follow these conditions:

  • 100% before the work starts
  • 60% during the sketch phase
  • After the rendering phase begins, the refund will be proportional to the work already completed, always being less than 50%

10. Client Conduct

The artist reserves the right to terminate the commission without a refund if the client:

  • Exhibits abusive behavior
  • Makes threats or engages in harassment
  • Applies excessive pressure outside of the agreed terms

11. AI Usage

The produced art may not be used for:

  • AI training
  • Machine learning datasets
  • Automated content generation

Without explicit written permission from the artist.

12. Acceptance of Terms

By commissioning the artist, the client declares that they have read, understood, and agreed to all the terms described above.
